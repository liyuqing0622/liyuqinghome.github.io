Yuqing Li(李雨晴)

I am currently an associate professor and a faculty member of DSP Lab, in the School of Cyber Science and Engineering at Wuhan University.
I was a Researcher at Theory Laboratory, Huawei Hong Kong Research Center from 2020-2022.
I was a Post-Doctoral Fellow in Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, advised by Prof. Bo Li from 2019-2020.
I obtained my PhD degree from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2019, advised by Prof. Xinbing Wang.
